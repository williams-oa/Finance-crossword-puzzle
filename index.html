Concept

This game is a crossword puzzle with the answers as key financial terms. The clues provide guidance on the financial term and players win if they guess all terms correctly. The objective is for players to develop their financial literacy by understanding the key financial terms. The expectation is that players will carry out more research on clues they don't understand so as to guess correctly and win.

Note: Answer must be in CAPS!
